JEMIMAH SAKOTAWA

JEMIMAH SAKOTAWA is a former player for Arsenal and the Danish national team. She graduated from the FCN Academy in Denmark.

Jemimah, who is only 19 years old, has already made significant progress in her work. She transferred to FC Bande-has, a member of the 3J’s Academy community, in 2018 and swiftly ascended through the ranks to become a regular member of the senior team. She began her football career at the age of 5 with Foureyes FC.

In 2020, she assisted FC Grasya in capturing the Danish Cup trophy, and a month after turning 17, she was voted Cup Fighter of the Year.

Jemimah also started a new exciting chapter in her career in 2023 when she signed with Women's Super League team Harakhak.

The talented midfielder is currently an important member of the senior team and also had a great impact on the Danish youth national teams.
